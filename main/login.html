Chain.Cloud SIGNER

Is used to:

Add your document to BlockChain. That can be useful if you want to generate 'timestamp' for your document that will last forever. And that unique 'timestamp' can never be removed.
Treat BlockChain technology as a single distributed database with no 'remove' or 'modify' functionality.

Proof of Existence/Timestamp

  1. You've written TV-series plot;
  2. You want to generate 'timestamp' for this document as a proof that you are the plot creator;
  3. Timestamp is then added to BlockChain; Your document is never transmitted to our servers so that we have no idea what contents it has;
  4. Now your ownership can be verified.

To verify ownership

  1. You need a full copy of the document (photo/photos);
  2. If your TV-series plot is lost -> no one will be able to restore it. Documents are never transmitted to BlockChain, only their hashes are;
  3. If you will change even single character in the plot -> you will not be able to verify it against old plot version. You need to add ALL versions of your plot in order to verify them later.
