A blockchain is a distributed database shared by all nodes

Traditionally data and information are shared through a ‘hub and spoke’ database model. A single institution, such as a bank, acts as the hub and disseminates information to us individually. But with BlockChain there is no ‘bank’ or central institution. Rather the account value is stored across the distributed network of thousands, or more, computers.

Blockchain technology has the power to create a more secure, fairer, more efficient system because there is no middle man.
